About me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Xi'an Jiaotong University, advised by Prof. Chao Shen. I am also very fortunate to collaborate closely with Prof. Shiqing Ma and Prof. Juan Zhai from Umass Amherst. Currently, I am a visiting Ph.D. student at Singapore Management University(SMU), under the supervision of Prof. Xiaofei Xie.

My research interests encompass Intersection of Software Engineering and AI (AI4SE, SE4AI) and Trustworthy AI. Specifically, I am actively involved in the following topics:

  • Effective and Efficient Red Teaming and Auditing for LLM Safety, Fairness, and Privacy;

  • Evaluation and Application of CodeLLM in Various Software Engineering Tasks;

  • Software Quality Assurance and Security Analysis for DL (particularly LLM) Infrastructure;
